In April of this year, I had the pleasure of giving the opening keynote at the Thinking Creatively Conference 10th Anniversary. Naturally, I was asked to speak about my experience on the Obama campaign in 2012. So, I put together a talk called Building Obama which went into detail on various projects I was a part of and what went into building the front-end of them. I also talked about our approach to optimise these products for the tens-of-millions of users that would come to barackobama.com.
